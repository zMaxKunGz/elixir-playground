    deal(deck, hand_size)

    Divides a deck into a hand and the remainder of the deck. The hand_size argument indicates how many card should be in hand.

    Examples 

    iex> deck = Cards.create_deck
iex> {hand, deck} = Cards.deal(deck, 1)
iex> hand
["Ace of Spades"]
